  1. prep work

    • preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
    • rest the butter to room temperature
    • wash your hands

  2. mix dry ingredient

    1. In a large mixing bowl add 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 cup of brown sugar, and 1 cup cocoa powder. Stir ingredients thoroughly.
    2. Add in 1 tablespoon of baking powder. Also 1 tsp of baking soda.
    3. Set aside.

  3. mix wet ingredients

    1. In a bowl, break 2 large eggs. Whisk until consistent.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract and half a teaspoon of salt
    3. Pour half cup of milk into wet mixture.

  4. combine mixtures

    1. Use stand mixer on low setting for three minutes, combining both mixtures.
    2. After the 5 minutes change setting to 8 and mix until combined.

  5. pour and bake

    1. Butter a 15x23 pan
    2. Pour mix into pan and level.
    3. Place into oven and lick edges of pan to ensure no burning.
    4. Bake for 45minutes.
